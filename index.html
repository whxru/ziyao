Bio

I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Southeast University in 2018 and my doctorate from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Southeast University and the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong in 2024. My advisor and co-advisor are Prof. Weiwei Wu and Prof. Jianping Wang. I am now a postdoc fellow at the City University of Hong Kong. My research interests include information freshness, wireless networking, reinforcement learning, and algorithm design.
